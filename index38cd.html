Financial questions

Is it possible to make a deposit without a cryptocurrency wallet?

Having an e-wallet is essential for depositing and withdrawing funds from Crypto Holding.Com. If you don’t have a cryptocurrency wallet yet, you must create one and transfer the crypto assets you intend to use for your deposit to your Crypto Holding.Com account.

I already have a cryptocurrency wallet. What cryptocurrencies do you accept?

We accept the following cryptocurrencies: BTC, USDT (TRC20), USDT (ERC20), USDT (BEP20), DOGE, LTC, BNB, TRX, XRP.

How to make a deposit?

In your personal account, you will find a new deposit tab. Select the investment package, blockchain coin, and enter the amount. Then follow the payment instructions.

How does the deposit process function?

Investors have the option to make a deposit, which becomes locked and generates daily income on business days (from Monday to Friday). The distribution of profits and the duration of deposit locking vary based on the chosen investment plan. Once the cycle concludes, your initial deposit will be released and can be withdrawn or reinvested to continue generating additional income.

What is the minimum amount I can deposit?

The minimum deposit amount is $50 for each of the accepted cryptocurrencies.

How long does it take for deposits to reflect in my account?

The processing time for deposits depends on the currency used and the amount of funds transferred. Typically, deposits are processed within 5-30 minutes. However, in certain instances, transaction completion may be delayed by several hours due to network congestion.

Can I reinvest my earnings?

We do not provide an automatic reinvestment option to compound your profits. However, you have the option to manually create a new deposit using funds from your account balance to reinvest your earnings.

When will Profits be credited to my account balance?

Profits are credited at 24-hour intervals starting from the moment you activate the deposit. For example, if you initiated the investment at 10:30 AM, your profit will be credited at 10:30 AM on the following business day.

Are there any deposit Fees?

No, we do not impose charges for deposits. However, please be aware of potential network fees. Networks may levy significant charges for transaction execution, so it's essential to consider this when making a deposit. Ensure the network fee is included in the transaction amount to deposit the desired sum into your account.

Is there a limit to the number of active deposits I can have?

No, there is no limit. You are free to have as many active deposits as you wish.

How can I withdraw my earnings?

To withdraw your earnings, follow these steps:
1. Log in to the User Area.
2. Navigate to the "Withdraw Funds" section in the left sidebar menu.

3. Select a cryptocurrency and the amount for the withdrawal.
4. Specify the e-wallet address to receive the funds.
We offer a variety of withdrawal methods: BTC, USDT (TRC20), USDT (BEP20), BNB, TRX, XRP.

What is the minimum amount I can withdraw?

The minimum withdrawal amount is $5 for each of the accepted cryptocurrencies.

How long does it take for withdrawals to be processed?

All withdrawal requests are processed instantly. However, the actual transaction execution time may vary due to network congestion or additional blockchain confirmations.

Are there any withdrawal fees?

No, all withdrawals are completely free of charge.

Can I withdraw into any cryptocurrency?

Withdrawals are limited to the same currency that your deposit is. For example, if you made a deposit in USDT BEP20, you could withdraw funds only to a USDT BEP20 address.

Is there a limit to the number of withdrawals I can make?

No, you are free to make as many separate withdrawals as you require.

Is there a limit on the withdrawal amount?

The withdrawal amount cannot exceed the available balance in your account.

How can I withdraw my deposit amount?

The deposit amount will be released and become available in your account at the end of the working cycle. The duration of the working cycle depends on the chosen investment plan.

Security Questions

How do we safeguard your personal and user data?

We employ a multi-layered security approach, incorporating unique protocols, DDoS protection, Two-factor authentication, and various other measures to ensure the confidential and secure use of our services.

How can I regain access to my account?

If you forget your password, you can easily recover it by clicking on the "Recover your login information" button and following the provided instructions. Should you encounter any issues during this process, please do not hesitate to contact our Support Team for further assistance.

How do I enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)?

To enable 2FA, follow these steps:
1. Log in to the User Area.
2. Navigate to the "Settings" section in the left sidebar menu.
3. In the "Settings" section, you'll find the "Security" tab.
4. Follow the provided steps to activate 2FA for your account.

How can I change my password?

To change your password, follow these steps:
1. Log in to the User Area.
2. Navigate to the "Settings" section in the left sidebar menu.
3. In the “Settings” section, you’ll find the option to change your password.

Where can I save and see my wallet addresses for withdrawals?

To save your wallet addresses, follow these steps:
1. Log in to the User Area.
2. Navigate to the "Settings" section in the left sidebar menu.
3. In the "Settings" section, you'll find the "Wallets" tab.
